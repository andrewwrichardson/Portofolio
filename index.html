Andrew's Portofolio Site

Andrew joined Arup in June 2013 having graduated from the University of Ulster with a MEng Civil Engineering with Distinction. Andrew has completed overseas assignments where he has developed a wealth of professional contacts whilst gaining experience on a wide range of projects to include Highways, Site Development, Airports and Rail. His experience working across disciplines and on local and international projects has given him experience in several codes of practice including British Standards, Eurocode and Canadian Standards.
