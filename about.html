About Me

Belfast Transport Hub – Main Works – Translink NI Feb 2020 – Present

Andrew coordinated and compiled the NEC 4 Scope document for the Transport Hub Mains Works. During the tender period he compiled the tender addendums and responses to tender clarifications.

Busconnects – Feb 2020- Present

This project involved the delivery of 12km of core bus corridor from Blanchardstown Shopping Centre to Ellis Quay. Andrew developed the road modelling strategy for the route coordinating this with the other consultants appointed to design the other Core Bus Corridors. As part of this project Andrew coordinated and compiled the Preliminary Design Report which forms part of the planning submission.

Belfast Transport Hub – Enabling Works – Translink NI Feb 2020 – July 2020

Andrew managed the review of the enabling works contractors design packages in conjunction with the ECC Supervisor.

UCD Future Campus Project Jan 2020- July 2021

This project involved the design of the Centre for Creativity and a world-class Centre for Future Learning providing an additional 22,500sqm of teaching space at UCD. Andrew developed the hardstanding design and coordinated the civil design with the landscape and building architects.
